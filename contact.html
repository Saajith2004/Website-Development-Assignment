Frequently Asked Questions

Are the recipes suitable for beginners? +
Absolutely! Our recipes are designed to be simple, easy to follow, and require minimal cooking experience.
Do I need a gym membership for the workouts? +
No! Our workout generator focuses on bodyweight exercises and routines you can do at home with minimal or no equipment.
How is my data used? +
We value your privacy. Your email for the newsletter and any feedback are stored locally on your device only (using localStorage) and are not sent to any server. This project is for demonstration purposes.
Can I contribute a recipe? +
We're not accepting external submissions right now, but we'd love to hear your ideas! Send us your feedback.
